Common questions

What counts as a "booked appointment"?

A qualified prospect who has confirmed a time to meet with your sales team — either in person, on a video call, or a phone consultation. We don't charge for no-shows or unqualified leads.

Does the $400 per appointment apply to every trade?

The per-appointment fee starts at $400 but may vary based on your trade, average job value, and market. We'll give you the exact number in your custom quote.

What's included in the setup fee?

Everything needed to get your Sales Intelligence running: training the AI on your services and pricing, integrating with your existing lead sources and CRM, building branded proposal templates, and onboarding your team. Most companies are live within 2 weeks.

Is there a minimum commitment?

We ask for a 3-month initial commitment so both sides have enough time to optimize performance. After that, it's month-to-month — you stay because the results speak for themselves.

Can I add Solvent, Bond, or Reactor later?

Absolutely. Most customers start with Catalyst and expand into additional products within 3 months. Multi-product pricing is available — ask about bundle discounts.

What if I have a large enterprise operation?

We work with operations of all sizes. For companies with 10+ reps, multiple locations, or complex needs, we offer custom enterprise packages with dedicated support, custom integrations, and volume pricing.
