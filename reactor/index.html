WHAT REACTOR DOES

The backend of your business,
fully automated.

Scheduling, dispatch, inventory, crew tracking — the stuff that eats your day. Reactor handles all of it.

01

Scheduling & dispatch

Reactor builds optimal daily schedules based on crew skills, location, job type, and availability. When things change — a permit comes through, a job runs long — it automatically re-optimizes and notifies everyone.

Auto-scheduling Route optimization Real-time re-dispatch
02

Inventory & materials

Tracks every piece of equipment and material across jobs, trucks, and warehouses. Reactor knows what's on each truck, what's running low, and auto-generates reorder alerts before you run out on a job site.

Truck-level tracking Auto-reorder Cost tracking
03

Crew performance

See which crews are hitting targets and which need support. Reactor tracks completion rates, time-on-site, customer feedback scores, and rework rates — so you can coach based on data, not gut feel.

Completion rates Quality scores Leaderboards
04

Job status & timeline

Every project has a real-time status board — from sale to permit to install to inspection to close-out. Your office team, field crews, and customers all see the same source of truth. No more "where are we on that job?" calls.
