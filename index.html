005 — The MethodProcedure

How the lab works.

Step 01

Distill

We live inside the problem until it gets simple. Permitting rules, crew logistics, sales psychology — mapped jurisdiction by jurisdiction, truck by truck, before a line of code.

Step 02

Synthesize

Design and engineering run as one loop, not a handoff. Agents, interfaces, and motion are built together — so the system is as considered as it is capable.

Step 03

Catalyze

Ship in days, not months. Customers are live on real data fast, and the agents compound — learning every pricing rule, AHJ quirk, and service area they touch.
